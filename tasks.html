Key Features

Add a new task

The user friendly design allows you to add a new task quickly and effortlessly. With clear and simple prompts with no distractions on the page. The fun about this is that you get to see your task instantly after creation, with a priority setting to help you focus on what is important.

View the task list

For tasks that still need to be completed regularly, our view task feature allows you view the list of all the tasks. This feature add a layer of organization to help you maintain clarity and control over your workload.

Keep up with your progress

Monitor the status of your tasks as you work through your list. Our progress tracking feature lets you see pending, and completed tasks at a glance, also a progression bar of the total completion percentage providing motivation and a sense of accomplishment.
